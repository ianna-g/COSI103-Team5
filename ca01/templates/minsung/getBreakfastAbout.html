About get_breakfast

Function:
Want breakfast? Don't know what to make? Use the get_breakfast program to get an original breakfast recipe. All you have to do is provide a list of ingredients you have and an AI chatbot, powered by OpenAI, will create a recipe for you!

How it works:
Once a list of ingredients is submitted to the Team 5 GPT web app API, additional text is prepended to the list in order to create a prompt.

User Input: salmon, bagel, eggs, spinach, peanut butter
Prepended text: Give me a creative breakfast dish name and a breakfast recipe using only the following ingredients:

The resulting prompt comes out to the following...
Give me a creative breakfast dish name and a breakfast recipe using only the following ingredients: salmon, bagel, eggs, spinach, peanut butter

After the prompt is created, it is sent to OpenAI's ChatGPT API to receive a response.
Once the response is received, the breakfast recipe is shown the the user.

Sample Output: 
Salmon-Egg Bagelwich

Ingredients:

-1 Bagel, cut in half
-2 eggs
-3 ounces of cooked salmon
-1 tablespoon of peanut butter
-1 handful of spinach

Instructions:

1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.

2. Spread the peanut butter on one side of the bagel and set aside.

3. Crack the eggs into the skillet and cook until the whites are set, about 2 minutes.

4. Place the cooked eggs on the peanut butter side of the bagel.

5. Top with the cooked salmon and spinach.

6. Place the other half of the bagel on top of the egg, salmon and spinach.

7. Cut in half and enjoy!
